## Assignment 4-reflection

technology and agriculture to address critical challenges in the fresh
produce supply chain. The use of vertical farming technology to create an
urban farming solution not only tackles the issues of limited options and
substandard quality but also aligns with the current trend of sustainable and
localized production. The incorporation of IoT solutions and Alibaba
Cloud services for real-time information is a testament to how technology
can enhance user experience and operational efficiency in the agricultural
sector.

I hope the speaker can provide more detailed explanation into strategies
for farming efficiently with technology and elaborate on more business
strategies to facilitate the expansion of their business. Specifically, I am
interested in how they plan to expand their target customer base
particularly individuals leading a healthy lifestyle in condominiums and
residential areas because I think it seems limited.

In conclusion, I-Farm clearly shows how innovative use of technology
and agriculture can revolutionize traditional industries for the better. Its
success not only in business performance but also in potential to serve as a
catalyst for positive environmental, social and economic change.
