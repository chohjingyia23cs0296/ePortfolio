# Technology and Information System (Year 1, Semester 1)

Welcome to the `technology_and_information_system` directory of my ePortfolio. This folder documents foundational assignments, industrial visits, and lab reflections from my first semester coursework.

## Repository Contents

*   **Assignment 2 - Reflection:** Insights and takeaways from the UTM Digital visit, highlighting the bridge between classroom theories and real-world technology applications.
*   **Assignment 3 - Reflection:** An overview of the industrial talk by Credence (Telekom Malaysia), focusing on analytics, cloud services, and tools like PostgreSQL and PowerBI.
*   **PC Assemble Lab Reflection:** A collaborative, hands-on experience building a computer from scratch, emphasizing teamwork and hardware comprehension.

## Personal Reflection

Looking back at these early assignments, this module was essential in establishing the groundwork for my current progression in Data Engineering at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia. The hands-on PC assembly lab provided a tangible understanding of hardware systems, which made it much easier to conceptualize virtualized environments and cloud infrastructure—such as AWS and Google Cloud—later in my studies. 

The industrial talks were particularly impactful. Hearing from companies like Credence sparked my initial curiosity in database management and analytics, driving my ongoing focus on building and optimizing SQL and NoSQL systems. While these assignments capture a great snapshot of my initial technical baseline, they represent just a fragment of my overall learning journey. Beyond these core systems, I am continuously exploring broader technological frameworks, advanced data pipeline architectures, and emerging industry practices to further develop my expertise.
