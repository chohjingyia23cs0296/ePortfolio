## Personal Reflection

Looking back at these early assignments, this module was essential in establishing the groundwork for my current progression in Data Engineering at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia. The hands-on PC assembly lab provided a tangible understanding of hardware systems, which made it much easier to conceptualize virtualized environments and cloud infrastructure—such as AWS and Google Cloud—later in my studies. 

The industrial talks and foundational design thinking methodologies were particularly impactful. Hearing about industry practices sparked my initial curiosity in database management and analytics, driving my ongoing focus on building and optimizing SQL and NoSQL systems. While these assignments capture a great snapshot of my initial technical baseline, they represent just a fragment of my overall learning journey. Beyond these core systems, I am continuously exploring broader technological frameworks, advanced data pipeline architectures, and emerging industry practices to further develop my expertise.
